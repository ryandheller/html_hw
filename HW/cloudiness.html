Cloudiness

This plot is comparing cloudiness levels from different locations across a range of lattitudes. There doesn't appear to be a strong correlation betweeen cloudiness and the lattitude of the location being tested. Although it does appear that most cities are either cloudless, or have a very high concentration of clouds, we see the data spread out more in the 20-80% range.
