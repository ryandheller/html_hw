Humidity

This plot is comparing humidity levels from different locations across a range of lattitudes. There doesn't appear to be a strong correlation betweeen humidity and the lattitude of the location being tested. Although it does appear that most locations tend to be fairly humid. We see only a couple responses with less than 20% humidity, and only a few more locations with less than 50% humidity. Most of the locations expereince humidity over 60%, regardless of the lattitude.
